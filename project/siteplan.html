Why I choose this name?

This name represents everyone with Sickle Cell anemia. The name represents their strength and courage. The word 'warrior' symbolizes bravery. It represents a community that provides warriors (and non-warriors) with the opportunity to learn about sickle cell anemia

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to educate people about Sickle Cell anemia and to clarify any misinformation they may have. It provides stories from other warriors and access to natural first-aid treatments.

How can I Sign up for the newsletter?

A sign up form is available on the homepage. Ensure you fill it out carefully. Once you click the sign-up button, you're all set!

How can I volunteer?

To volunteer, scroll to the bottom of the page. Under "Advocacy", select the opportunity you would like to volunteer for.

How can I get in touch with you?

Check out our social media links at the bottom of the page or click on the red "contact" button. If you're using a mobile phone, open the hamburger menu and select "contact"
